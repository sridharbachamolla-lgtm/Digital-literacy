# Module Summaries

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

I created an infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. The infographic was designed to spread awareness among students about responsible use of technology.

**Tool Used:** Canva

---

## Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

I created and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional online presence and will be useful for future internships and projects.

**Platforms Used:** GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle

---

## Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms

I completed the “Solve Me First” challenge on HackerRank and created a Google Form called “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” with five questions.

**Platforms Used:** HackerRank, Google Forms, Google Sheets

---

## Task 4 – Professional Email and Etiquette Guide

I wrote two professional emails and prepared a social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist. This task helped me understand the importance of professional communication.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness and Prevention

I created a case study on UPI and online payment fraud and prepared a “Stay Safe Online” checklist with prevention tips for students.
